Video Content

Recently, I completed an internship with TUTV Media Lab. TUTV Media Lab is The University of Tulsa's on-campus, student-led weekly news show. As part of my internship, I created packages, or short videos about any topic, to air on the show. Check below to see some of the packages I created.

Box of Joy

For this package, my friends and I went shopping for our Boxes of Joy, which is a program put on by Cross Catholic Outreach.

Anime Opinions

In this package, I interviewed my friends about their opinions on anime. It is interesting to me that people either seem to love or hate the genre, and I wanted to see if I could figure out why. These interviews were conducted over Zoom due to COVID-19.
